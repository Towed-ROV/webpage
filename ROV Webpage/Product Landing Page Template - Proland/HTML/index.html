Basic Features

GPS Tracking

The ROV includes GPS tracker and this can be used to save the position data of diving sessions

Sonar Imaging

Our ROV also has side scan sonars to get the live scan the ocean bed

Camera Recording

With the help of the camera mounted on the ROV, the ROV also receives live video stream from underwater

Anti-Collision

The control system on the ROV can perform sea floor tracking and adjust the depth keeping a certain offset from the sea bed

Depth Control

The contol system can also be used to maintain the ROV at a certain depth from the sea surface

Computer Vision

The ROV is able to detect and classify trash found underwater using machine learning
